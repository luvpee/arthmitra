# ArthMitra — Vision

## What is ArthMitra?
ArthMitra (Arth = Money, Mitra = Friend in Hindi) is a personal AI Chartered Accountant for Indian students and middle-class families. It acts as a financial companion that knows your complete financial history and gives personalised advice — not generic tips.

## The Problem It Solves
- CAs are expensive and inaccessible for middle-class Indians
- Generic finance apps track money but don't think
- ChatGPT gives generic advice with no memory of your situation
- No existing app automatically reads bank statements AND gives contextual AI advice

## Who It's For
Primary: Indian college students (18-24) with limited income, managing pocket money, college fees, and first investments
Secondary: Middle-class Indian families wanting simple financial guidance

## Why It's Different From ChatGPT
- Persistent memory — remembers every transaction forever across sessions
- Auto-ingests bank statements via PDF upload
- Gives advice based on YOUR actual data, not generic knowledge
- Proactive alerts — warns you before problems happen, not after
- Multi-agent system — specialized agents for different financial needs

## Core Value Proposition
"The difference between ArthMitra and ChatGPT is like the difference between a doctor who knows your complete medical history versus a random doctor you meet for the first time every visit."
